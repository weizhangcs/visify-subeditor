Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Aimu Online Subtitle Editor ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Our extension collects the following information:

  • The URL of the current tab when you click the extension icon (to enable subtitle editing functionality)
  • Browser language settings (to determine which service endpoint to use)

Use of Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Direct you to the appropriate service endpoint based on your location
  • Enable subtitle editing functionality for the video URL you're accessing

Data Storage

The extension does not store any personal information locally or remotely. All processing is done in real-time.

Third-Party Services

Our extension interacts with the following domains:

  • aimu.app
  • aimu-app.com

These interactions are necessary for the core functionality of our service.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what information is being collected
  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Contact us with any questions about your privacy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the Chrome Web Store or through the extension itself.
